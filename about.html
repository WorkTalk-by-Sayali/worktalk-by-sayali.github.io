About

Hi, I'm Sayali.

I'm an HR professional who enjoys working through the practical, often messy problems that come with building good people practices.

My work spans designing performance management systems, planning workforce needs, and making sure pay stays fair and competitive. Work Talk is where I write up the projects I've worked on: what the problem actually looked like, what approach worked, what didn't, and what I'd do differently next time.

The goal isn't to give you a textbook definition — it's to share the kind of practical detail I wish I'd had when I was figuring these things out myself. If you're an HR practitioner, a founder building your first people function, or just curious about how these systems get built in the real world, I hope something here is useful to you.
